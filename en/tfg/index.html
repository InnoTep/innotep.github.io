Final Degree and Master's Thesis Projects

Join InnoTep and develop your BSc/MSc thesis in innovative projects

Project Opportunities for Students

At InnoTep we offer Final Degree Projects (BSc Thesis) and Master’s Thesis (MSc Thesis) focused on technological innovation applied to the fields of health, security, privacy, and education.

Working with our group will allow you to participate in real projects with social impact, collaborate internationally, apply cutting-edge technologies, and receive specialized mentoring.

🎯 Available Projects

These are specific BSc/MSc thesis proposals you can develop with InnoTep. We are also open to student proposals that align with our research lines.

Do you have your own idea? If you have a proposal that aligns with our research lines, we’d be delighted to hear it!

🔒 Audit System and Adversarial Injection Detection in Clinical AI

Area: AI Cybersecurity | Natural Language Processing | Healthcare Systems

Design and implementation of a “Security Gateway” for healthcare environments capable of detecting data poisoning attacks in AI systems processing clinical text. The project implements the MEDLEY defense framework using a heterogeneous model ensemble that detects anomalies through prediction discrepancy.

Technologies: Python, Transformers, BERT, LLMs, FastAPI, Monitoring dashboard

Level: BSc Thesis
Duration: 4-6 months

Why do your BSc/MSc Thesis with InnoTep?

  • 🔬 Research with purpose: Participate in real projects with social impact
  • 🌍 International collaboration: Work in a network with universities and organizations from Europe, Africa, and Latin America
  • 💡 Cutting-edge technology: Apply the latest technologies in AI, IoT, virtual reality, human-computer interaction, and more
  • 👥 Specialized mentoring: Count on the support of expert researchers in each area
  • 📚 Publications: Possibility to contribute to scientific publications

Requirements and Skills

For BSc Thesis

  • Solid programming knowledge (Python, JavaScript, Java, etc.)
  • Interest in applied research
  • Autonomous and team work capability
  • Curiosity about emerging technologies

For MSc Thesis

  • Previous experience in software development or research
  • Command of research methodologies
  • Critical analysis capability
  • Advanced knowledge in the area of specialization

Application Process

  1. Contact us: Send an email to gi.innotep@upm.es with:

    • Your updated CV
    • Academic transcript
    • Motivation letter explaining your interest
    • Project you’re interested in or your own proposal

  2. Initial interview: We’ll meet with you to understand your interests and skills

  3. Project assignment: We’ll propose a project according to your profile and interests

  4. Start working: Let’s start developing your BSc/MSc thesis!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do my BSc/MSc thesis in English?
Yes, we work in an international environment and you can develop your project in English.

Is it mandatory to have prior knowledge in the specific area?
Not necessarily. What matters is your motivation and learning ability. We will provide the necessary training.

How long does it take to develop a BSc/MSc thesis?
Normally between 4-6 months for BSc and 6-9 months for MSc, depending on the project scope.

Can I work remotely?
Yes, we adapt the work modality to your needs, although some projects may require occasional on-site presence.

Is there a possibility of continuity after the BSc/MSc thesis?
Yes, many of our students continue collaborating with the group in research projects, PhDs, or predoctoral contracts.
